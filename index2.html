I DON'T BELIEVE IN ANARCHY
"Punk may have started in New York and London, but the extremely famous and famously extreme existential cult Siberian 80's band GrOb (Grazhdanskaya Oborona, or Civic Defense) and it's visionary leader Yegor Letov created something entirely different than the western rock-n-roll that had inspired it."

I Don't Believe in Anarchy (an independent feature documentary) has been released in Russia (original title: Здорово и Вечно) in November 2014 to critical acclaim and commercial success. It will have its international premiere in 2015.
